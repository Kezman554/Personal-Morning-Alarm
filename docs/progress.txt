2026-06-06  Shake detection rewrite + crash fix + content screens verified
  - ROOT CAUSE of recurring shake pain: delta-between-samples detection is
    rate-sensitive and aliases against the vibration motor — under-registered real
    shakes. Rewrote ShakeChallenge to FORCE-based: |accel magnitude - gravity| >
    threshold (3.5 m/s^2), independent of sample rate and orientation
  - CRASH fix: had switched to SENSOR_DELAY_FASTEST, which needs
    HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS on API 31+ and threw SecurityException ->
    AlarmDismissalActivity crashed on resume (this, not a vibration self-trigger,
    was the "alarm turned itself off" on 06-05 AND 06-06). Reverted to
    SENSOR_DELAY_GAME (~50Hz, no permission)
  - Removed Stage 1 vibration from AlarmService: force-based detection would read
    the motor as shaking; sound-only also keeps Stage 1 gentle (PRD). Also removed
    the activity's shake haptic pulses (same feedback risk)
  - Notification tap mid-shake reset progress: AlarmService full-screen intent used
    FLAG_ACTIVITY_CLEAR_TASK, recreating the activity. Removed it; singleTask now
    reuses the instance. Notification text -> "Shake your phone to dismiss"
  - VERIFIED on device (Nick): shake felt right ("small deliberate effort"); diag
    showed ~50Hz, 0.0 m/s^2 at rest (no false trigger), 53-103 m/s^2 while shaking
    (threshold 3.5 has big headroom). Quote + Stretch content screens both appeared.
    Full Stage 1 -> content -> Stage 2 flow works end to end
  - Diagnostics logging removed after tuning
